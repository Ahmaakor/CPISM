Bora Bora Beach, French Polynesia

Overview

Bora Bora Beach is known for its crystal-clear waters, luxurious resorts, and breathtaking views.

Best Time to Visit

April to November – Dry season with pleasant weather.

Activities & Attractions

  • Snorkeling & Scuba Diving
  • Luxury Overwater Bungalows
  • Sunset Cruises
  • Hiking Mount Otemanu

Nearby Hotels & Restaurants

Recommended hotels: St. Regis Bora Bora, Four Seasons Resort

How to Get There

Fly to Bora Bora Airport, then take a boat to the island.

Weather Conditions

Average temperature: 25-30°C (77-86°F)
